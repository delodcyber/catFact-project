About CatFacts

Built and Designed by John Adeloye - for people who thought "a cat is just a cat" until reality scratched back.

The idea behind it

Growing up, I only knew there were cats. Just cats. No categories, no breeds, no fancy names - just cats doing cat things. It wasn't until later that I realized something simple but easy to miss: cats are not all the same.

And honestly, a lot of people are still there. Surrounded by cats online, in homes, in photos - without ever really knowing the differences between them or the names behind those faces.

That's why CatFacts exists. A place where cat lovers (and accidental cat scrollers) can explore breeds, learn facts, and slowly realize they've been underestimating cats their whole lives.

What you can do here

Breed Explorer

Browse cat breeds with details on temperament, origin, weight, and lifespan.

Random Cat Facts

Because cats deserve a dedicated trivia department.

Learning Through Curiosity

Small discoveries that quietly turn "just browsing" into "wait... there are how many breeds?"

What's next

More personalization. Better filtering. Favorite breeds that actually remember your taste. Maybe even letting users build their own "cat watchlist" instead of endlessly scrolling like a confused kitten.
